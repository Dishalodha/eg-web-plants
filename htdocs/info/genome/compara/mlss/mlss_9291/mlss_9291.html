Prunus persica vs Vitis vinifera LastZ alignments

Prunus persica (Prunus persica, GCA_000346465.1) and Vitis vinifera (Vitis vinifera, IGGP_12x) were aligned using the LastZ alignment algorithm (LastZ) in Ensembl release 76. Prunus persica was used as the reference species. After running LastZ, the raw LastZ alignment blocks are chained according to their location in both genomes. During the final netting process, the best sub-chain is chosen in each region on the reference species.
