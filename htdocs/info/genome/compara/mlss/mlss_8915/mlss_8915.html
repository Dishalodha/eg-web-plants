Sorghum bicolor vs Setaria italica LastZ alignments

Sorghum bicolor (Sorghum bicolor, Sorbi1) and Setaria italica (Setaria italica, JGIv2.0) were aligned using the LastZ alignment algorithm (LastZ) in Ensembl release 76. Sorghum bicolor was used as the reference species. After running LastZ, the raw LastZ alignment blocks are chained according to their location in both genomes. During the final netting process, the best sub-chain is chosen in each region on the reference species.
